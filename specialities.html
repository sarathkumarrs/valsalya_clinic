(MBBS,DCH)

Paediatrics

A paediatrics specialist is a medical doctor who focuses on the health and well-being of infants, children, and adolescents. They diagnose and treat a wide range of childhood illnesses, monitor growth and development, provide vaccinations, and guide parents on nutrition, behavior, and preventive care. Paediatricians also manage chronic conditions such as asthma, allergies, and developmental disorders, ensuring children receive comprehensive and compassionate care at every stage of growth. Their goal is to promote healthy development and support families in raising happy, healthy children.
